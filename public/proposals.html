Call for Talks and Workshops

We'd love for everyone and anyone who is doing amazing things with Node.js to submit a presentation proposal to EmpireNode 2014. The conference itself is October 27th, 2014. If you're coming from out of town we should be able to cover all of your travel (unless you live in space or something crazy like that).

We are serious when we say EVERYONE AND ANYONE. If you've never given a talk before that's EVEN MORE reason to submit a proposal here now. If you have any questions about what we're looking for please just drop us an email at speakers@empirenode.org
